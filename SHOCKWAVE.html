Free and Open Source C++ Engine

What is Shockwave?

the Shockwave Engine(not to be confused with the now-defunct Adobe Shockwave; formerly known as TOEPMO) is a C++ 2D/3D game engine made by cooldude2144 because every other engine pissed him off.

FAQ

What platforms will Shockwave support

Windows, Linux, HTML5, Android* and Xbox One/Series X/S (and maybe PlayStation?)

PC, Web and Android are only supported by Shockwave-Pixel

How does it work?

Shockwave currently has two versions, Shockwave-Pixel and Shockwave-Polygon. (both are seperate)

Is it Open-Source?

Yes.

Will it piss me off?

Shockwave is designed to be friendly to developers and will never let you down with compiler errors.

Why is it called Shockwave?

Well technically, the name Shockwave sounds cool.
