What the fuck is a TOEPMO?

TOEPMO [spelled as tope-moh] is a C++ 2D/3D game engine made by cooldude2144 because every other engine pissed him off.

FAQ

What platforms will TOEPMO support

Windows, Linux, HTML5, Android* and Xbox One/Series X/S

PC, Web and Android are only supported by TOEPMO-3d

How does it work?

TOEPMO currently has two versions, TOEPMO-2d and TOEPMO-3d. (both are seperate)

Is it Open-Source?

Yes. (only for Tope2d).

Will it piss me off?

TOEPMO is designed to be friendly to developers and will never let you down with compiler errors.

Why is it called TOEPMO?

Well, it's because of my personal experience with game engines that made me create this engine in the first place.
